[h1]Stricter Empire Obscurity[/h1]
Savegame-compatible.

Changing the Rule "Empire Obscurity", making it stricter.

[h2]OLD[/h2]
Empire titles get destroyed when the holder holds less than 20% of the title's
de jure counties.

[h2]NEW[/h2]
Empire and Kingdom titles get destroyed when the holder holds less than 50% of
the title's de jure counties.
