[b]Stricter Empire Obscurity[/b]
Savegame-compatible.

Changing the Rule "Empire Obscurity", making it stricter.

[quote][b]OLD[/b]
Empire titles get destroyed when the holder holds less than 20% of the title's de jure counties.[/quote]

[quote][b]NEW[/b]
Empire and Kingdom titles get destroyed when the holder holds less than 50% of the title's de jure counties.[/quote]

It is a very subtle change and realms are pretty balanced at start, but if you load this mod into an already established game you mayhaps see some funny collapses.
